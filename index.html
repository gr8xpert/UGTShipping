Who We Are

A Legacy of Maritime Excellence

Based in Salalah, Dhofar, AL UROOJ GENERAL TRADE & SHIPPING is a diversified trading and maritime services company operating across the Sultanate of Oman.

With deep expertise in commodities, animal feed, and vessel brokerage, we serve as a one-stop partner for businesses navigating the region's trade and shipping landscape.

What We Do

Our Core Services

Comprehensive trade and maritime solutions tailored to meet the demands of regional and international markets.

Commodities Trading

Expert procurement and distribution of essential global commodities, ensuring seamless supply chain integration.

Animal Feed & Fodder

Specialized trading of high-quality animal feed and fodder, supporting the agricultural backbone of Dhofar.

Ship Handling & Provisions

Comprehensive port services including crew welfare, deck/engine supplies, and high-standard provisions.

Vessel Sale & Purchase

Professional brokerage services for the acquisition and disposal of commercial vessels in the regional market.

Demolition Brokerage

Reliable scrap vessel brokerage, managing end-of-life vessel transactions with environmental compliance.

Shipping Consultancy

Strategic advisory for maritime operations, port regulations, and trade optimization within Oman.
